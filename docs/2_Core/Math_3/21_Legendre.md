$$

## Legendre Series

Similar to [Fourier Series](17_Fourier_Series.md), Any function $f(x)$ can be represented as

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n P_n(x) \\
a_n &= \frac{2n+1}{2} \int_{-1}^1 f(x) P_n(x) dx
\end{aligned}
$$

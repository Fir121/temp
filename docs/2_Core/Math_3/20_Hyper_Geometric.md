## Standard Form

$$
x(1-x) y'' +
\Big[c - (a+b+1)x \Big]y' -
(ab)y = 0
$$

where $a, b, c$ are real constants

$x=0, x=1$ are the regular singular points of equation

By Frobenius Series method, at regular singular points, we get 2 initial roots

- $m=0$
- $m=1-c$

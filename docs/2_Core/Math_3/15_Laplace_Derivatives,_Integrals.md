## Derivatives

$$
\begin{aligned}
L\{ f'(t) \} &= s F(s) - f(0) \\
L\{ f''(t) \} &= s^2 F(s) - sf(0) - f'(0) \\
\Big( L\{ f(t) \} &= F(s) \Big)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(0) &= \{ f(t) \}_{t = 0} \\
f'(0) &= \left\{ \frac{d f(t)}{dt} \right\}_{t = 0} \\
f'(t) &= \frac{df}{dx}; f''(t) = \frac{d^2f}{dx^2}
\end{aligned}
$$

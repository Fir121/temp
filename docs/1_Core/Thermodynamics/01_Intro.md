## Pressure

$$
\begin{aligned}
P
&= \frac F A \\
&= \rho g h \\
P_\text{abs} 
&= P_\text{atm} + P_\text{gage} \\
&= P_\text{atm} - P_\text{vac} \\
1 \text{ bar} &= 100 \text{ kPa}
\end{aligned}
$$

## Manometer

Denser fluid will be below

$$
\begin{aligned}
P_\text{a} &= P_\text{b} \\
P_{g_1} + \rho_1 g h_1
&= P_{g_2} + \rho_2 g h_2
\end{aligned}
$$
